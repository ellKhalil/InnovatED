Company Profile Brochure/Readers Digest

At InnovatED, our mission is to create a vibrant research ecosystem by connecting organizations that require research with talented researchers and students. Here's how our platform works:

For Researchers

At InnovatED, we value the essential role researchers play in generating new knowledge and innovation. We understand that funding and support are key factors that determine the quality and output of research work. That's why we've created a platform that connects you with organizations that are passionate about investing in research.

Why Choose InnovatED?
  • Access to Funding: We have a wide range of organizations willing to fund research in various fields. Whether you are an experienced researcher or a student starting your research journey, there are numerous opportunities for you.
  • Collaboration Opportunities: Work with organizations closely related to your research field, get industry insights, and make your research more relevant and impactful.
  • Visibility for Your Work: All research work funded through InnovatED will be published on our platform, offering you a wider audience, recognition, and opportunities for future collaborations.
  • Supportive Community: Join a community of researchers, share ideas, ask questions, and learn from each other.

    • Getting Started
    • Register as a researcher on InnovatED. Provide your credentials, field of study, and research interests.
    • Browse the available funded research topics or propose a new topic.
    • Apply for funding and start your research once your application is approved.

    For Organizations

    InnovatED offers a unique opportunity for organizations to directly fund and influence the direction of research in areas that matter most to them. By becoming a funding partner, you have the chance to drive innovation and growth in your field.

    Why Partner with InnovatED?
    • Impact: Your funding directly influences the progression and output of impactful research in your field.
    • Brand Visibility: As a funding partner, your organizationâ€™s name and logo will be associated with the research projects you support, increasing your visibility in the academic and industry community.
    • Talent Acquisition: Get to know talented researchers who are potential future employees or collaborators.
    • Stay Ahead of the Curve: Get early access to the latest research findings in your field.

      • How to Become a Funding Partner
      • Register as a researcher on InnovatED. Provide your credentials, field of study, and research interests.
      • Review researcher proposals and choose which ones you want to fund based on your interests and strategic goals.
      • Track the progress of your funded research projects and connect with researchers directly through InnovatED.
